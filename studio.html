Series Strategy
Episodes alternate between Arc 1 and Arc 2 so the audience experiences the full buyer journey over time. The Prospect and Franchesca are the only two characters who appear in both arcs -- this mirrors what actually happens in a franchise relationship. The contrast between who shows up before versus after the check clears is the central emotional engine of the series.
