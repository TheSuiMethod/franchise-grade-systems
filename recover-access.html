Lost access to your tools?

If you cleared your browser data, switched devices, or cannot access tools you previously purchased, we can restore your access quickly.

Why this happens

Your tool access is stored in your browser for privacy and security. If you clear your browsing data, use a different browser, or switch to a new device, that stored access is removed.

This does not affect your purchase. You paid for lifetime access to your tools, and we will get you back in.

Request access recovery

Send us a quick email with the details below and we will restore your access within one business day.

Email Access Recovery Request

Or email hello@franchisegradesystems.com directly with this information:

  • Your name (as it appeared on the purchase)
  • The email address you used at checkout
  • Which product(s) you purchased
  • Approximate date of purchase
Response time: within 1 business day

Prevent this in the future

After we restore your access, use the "Copy My Access Links" or "Email Links to Myself" buttons on your confirmation page. This gives you a backup you can use anytime, from any device.
