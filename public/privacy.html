Privacy Policy

Effective: February 2026

Information We Collect

When you make a purchase, we collect your name, email address, and payment information through our payment processor, Stripe. Stripe handles all payment data directly - we never see or store your full credit card number.

When you use the AI FDD Analyzer, you paste text from your Franchise Disclosure Document into our tool. This text is processed in real-time by our AI analysis engine to generate your report.

How We Use Your Information

Your email address is used to deliver your purchase (PDF reports) and for transactional communications related to your order. We do not send marketing emails unless you explicitly opt in.

FDD text you paste into the analyzer is used solely to generate your analysis. It is sent to our AI processing service (Anthropic's Claude API) for analysis and is not stored by us after your report is generated. Anthropic's commercial API does not use API-submitted data for model training.

AI Data Processing - Summary

We use Anthropic's Claude commercial API to process FDD text for analysis. Here is how your data is handled:

Processing: Your FDD text is sent to Anthropic's API via encrypted HTTPS connection for real-time analysis only. The text is used to generate your report and is not retained by our systems.

No model training: Anthropic's commercial API terms explicitly state that data submitted through the API is not used to train or improve their AI models. This applies to all text you submit through our tools.

Anthropic's data retention: Per Anthropic's commercial API terms, input and output data may be retained for up to 30 days for trust and safety purposes, after which it is deleted. This retention is for abuse monitoring only and data is not used for any other purpose.

No storage on our servers: We do not store your FDD text on our servers. Once your analysis is complete and your PDF report is generated, the submitted text is not retained by Franchise-Grade Systems.

For questions about Anthropic's data practices, refer to their commercial API terms and privacy policy.

Data Retention

Payment records are retained as required by tax and accounting regulations. Your FDD text is not stored after your analysis session ends. Your generated PDF report is available for download during your session.

Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services to operate our business:

Stripe (stripe.com) - payment processing. Stripe's privacy policy governs how your payment data is handled.

Anthropic (anthropic.com) - AI analysis processing. FDD text is sent to Anthropic's API for analysis. Per Anthropic's data policy, API submissions are not used to train AI models.

Vercel (vercel.com) - website hosting.

Cookies

We use minimal cookies required for site functionality and payment processing. We do not use advertising cookies or tracking pixels.

Your Rights

You may request deletion of your personal information by contacting us. Note that we may need to retain certain records for legal and accounting purposes.

Security

All data is transmitted over encrypted HTTPS connections. Payment processing is handled by Stripe, which is PCI DSS Level 1 certified. We do not store sensitive payment information on our servers.

Children's Privacy

Our services are not directed to individuals under 18. We do not knowingly collect information from children.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy as our services evolve. Material changes will be noted with an updated effective date.

Contact

For privacy-related questions, contact us through our website at franchisegradesystems.com.
