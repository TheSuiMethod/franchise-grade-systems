The franchise model is not the only way to build a systemized business.

Franchise buyers pay for one thing: a proven system. Standard operating procedures, training manuals, marketing playbooks, financial benchmarks. The "business in a box."

But that box comes with a price tag that never stops growing. Royalties, brand fund fees, technology fees, mandatory vendor markups. Over a 10-year franchise term, these costs add up fast.

*Based on $300,000 annual gross revenue. Higher-revenue franchises pay significantly more. Fee ranges sourced from FTC-mandated FDD disclosures across franchise systems.
