We never see, store, or process your credit card information. All payments are handled by Stripe, which is certified PCI-DSS Level 1 â€” the highest level of payment security certification available. Your card details go directly from your browser to Stripe's servers.

FDD Text Processing

Your FDD text is processed and discarded.

When you paste your FDD text into our analyzer, here's exactly what happens:

1. Your FDD text is sent via encrypted HTTPS to our analysis API.
2. The API sends the text to Anthropic's Claude API for analysis.
3. Claude returns the analysis results.
4. Your report is generated and delivered to you.
5. Your FDD text is not saved to any database, file system, or cache on our servers.

We do not store your FDD text after analysis. Period. There is no "account" where your documents live, no file storage, and no way for us to retrieve your FDD text after your session ends.

AI Processing

Anthropic's commercial API terms protect your data.

We use Anthropic's Claude API under their commercial terms of service. Under these terms:

No model training: Anthropic does not use data submitted through their commercial API to train their AI models. Your FDD text will never be used to teach an AI system.

Limited retention: Anthropic may retain API inputs for up to 30 days solely for trust and safety monitoring (detecting abuse and policy violations). After that window, inputs are deleted. This retention is governed by Anthropic's privacy policy.

No human review of content: Under normal circumstances, Anthropic does not review the content of API requests. Content is only reviewed if their automated systems flag a potential trust and safety violation.

Bottom line: Your FDD text is processed through an encrypted connection, analyzed by AI that won't train on it, and is not stored on our servers. The only retention is Anthropic's standard 30-day trust and safety window, after which it's deleted.
Website & Hosting

Hosted on Vercel with HTTPS everywhere.

The Franchise-Grade Systems website is hosted on Vercel, which provides automatic HTTPS/TLS encryption for all pages and API routes. All data transmitted between your browser and our servers is encrypted in transit.

We use no tracking cookies, no third-party analytics services, and no advertising pixels. Your browsing behavior on our site is not shared with or sold to anyone.

If you subscribe to our email list, your email address is stored by Kit (formerly ConvertKit) under their privacy policy. You can unsubscribe at any time using the link in any email. We do not share your email address with any other service or third party.

Expert Review Sessions

1-on-1 sessions are confidential.

Information shared during Expert Review sessions is treated as confidential. We do not record sessions without your explicit consent. Notes taken during the session are used solely to prepare your written action plan and are not shared with any third party.

Scheduling is handled by Calendly under their privacy policy.
